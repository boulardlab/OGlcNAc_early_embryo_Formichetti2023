Perturbing nuclear glycosylation in the mouse preimplantation embryo slows down embryonic growth

This website shows the results of the analyses of RNA-Seq data for paper "Perturbing nuclear glycosylation in the mouse preimplantation embryo slows down embryonic growth" ([Formichetti et al. 2023](link to biorxiv to add])). Source Rmd code is in github repository.

2-cell embryos

  • Analysis of publicly available Smart-Seq data of pre-EGA and post-EGA 2-cell embryos
  • Filtering steps and clustering of 2-cell embryos injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Analysis of differential gene expression in 2-cell embryos injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Gene set enrichment analysis in 2-cell embryos injected with Btgh
  • Analysis of retrotransposons expression in 2-cell embryos injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected

Morulae

  • Filtering steps and clustering of morulae injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Analysis of differential gene expression in morulae injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Gene set enrichment analysis in morulae injected with Btgh
  • Analysis of retrotransposons expression in morulae injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected

Blastocysts

  • Filtering steps and clustering of blastocysts injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Analysis of differential gene expression in blastocysts injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected
  • Gene set enrichment analysis in blastocysts injected with Btgh
  • Comparison of GSEA results for morulae and blastocysts injected with Btgh
  • Dynamics of differentially expressed genes (DEGs) at the three preimplantation stages upon Btgh injection
  • PCA of the single embryos from the three preimplantation stages non-injected or injected with Btgh/dBtgh
  • Allelic-specific analysis of RNA-Seq counts from FVB/PWD F1 blastocysts
  • Analysis of retrotransposons expression in blastocysts injected with Btgh/dBtgh or non-injected

  • Analysis of differential gene expression in E7 embryos injected with Btgh or dBtgh

  • Counts of Btgh/dBtgh mRNA molecules, Ogt and Oga transcripts at all analyzed embryonic stages after Btgh injection

Publicly available mRNA-Seq data spanning mouse early development (GSE66582 and GSE76505, only wild-type embryos)

  • Ogt and Oga transcript isoforms across early development
  • Analysis of differential gene expression between consecutive mouse preimplantation stages
  • Dynamics across preimplantation stages of genes coherently deregulated upon nuclear O-GlcNAc removal in 2-cell embryos, morulae and blastocysts
  • Dynamics across early development of DNAme enzymes as well as DEGs found in single E7 epiblasts from Btgh-inj. embryos
  • Dynamics across preimplantation stages of genes associated with PC1/2 of PCA of 2-cell embryos, morulae and blastocysts from our study
  • Analysis of retrotransposons expression across preimplantation stages
